Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a project typically take?

Starter projects take 2-4 weeks, Growth packages 4-8 weeks, and Enterprise projects 8-16 weeks depending on complexity.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We typically split payments into 40% upfront, 30% at midpoint, and 30% on delivery. Custom arrangements are possible for larger projects.

What happens after the support period ends?

You can extend support with a monthly retainer (from €300/month) or purchase ad-hoc support hours as needed.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely. We build with scalability in mind. You can always add features or upgrade to a higher tier as your business grows.
