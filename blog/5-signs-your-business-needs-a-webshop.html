5 Signs Your Business Needs a Webshop in 2026

By Saspire Team • May 29, 2026 • 5 min read

The world has gone digital, yet many Belgian B2B companies still process orders via phone calls, email threads, and fax machines. If any of the following signs sound familiar, it's time to invest in a proper webshop.

1. Your Sales Team Spends More Time on Admin Than Selling

If your reps are manually entering orders, looking up stock levels, or re-sending price lists, they're not selling. A webshop automates order entry, stock visibility, and pricing — freeing your team to focus on relationships and upselling.

2. Customers Ask "Can I Just Order Online?"

When your buyers — especially younger procurement managers — start asking for online ordering, that's a signal you're losing ground to competitors who already offer it. In 2026, B2B buyers expect the same convenience they get as B2C consumers.

3. Your PDF Catalog Is Outdated Before It's Printed

Physical catalogs are expensive and static. By the time they reach your customer, prices may have changed or products gone out of stock. A digital product catalog updates in real-time and can be searched, filtered, and ordered from directly.

4. You're Losing Orders Outside Business Hours

A webshop works 24/7. If your customers operate in different time zones, or simply want to reorder at 9 PM, they can — without waiting for your office to open. Studies show that 67% of B2B purchases happen outside traditional working hours.

5. You Can't Track What's Working

Without digital infrastructure, you have no visibility into which products are trending, which customers are about to churn, or where your revenue actually comes from. A webshop with analytics gives you a dashboard of your entire commercial operation.
